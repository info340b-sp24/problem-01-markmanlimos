Ash Ketchum

Ash Ketchum is the main character of Pokemon. Throughout the past 2 decades, he has strived to become the Pokemon champion of every region he has visited.

    Reasons I admire Ash:
  • Even though he loses every time he challenges the champion, he continues to work to win in the next region
  • He never takes the easy way out and often starts each region with weaker Pokemon than he ended the last region with
  • He has unwaivering determination in the face of adversity throughout the show
